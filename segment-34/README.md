# Segment 34 Watchface
Watchface for Amazfit GTR Mini and for other round ZeppOS watch.

![demo](./demo-gtr-mini.png)

## Features

**Main features**
Modern watchface with a unique segmented font and comprehensive data: weather (current weather, sunrise/sunset, moon phase), date, and essential health & activity stats (heart rate, calories, sleep, steps), plus device status indicators.

**Original watchface**  
This is almost a copy of great and famous [Segment34 MkII by @ludw](https://github.com/ludw/Segment34mkII) for Garmin watches ([Garmin Store](https://apps.garmin.com/apps/aa85d03d-ab89-4e06-b8c6-71a014198593)).

**Model compatibility:** Amazfit GTR Mini, Amazfit GTR 4 and all other round ZeppOS watches

**Note: Time text requires devices with ZeppOS 2 and higher and is not compatible with older models like the GTR 3 and GTR 3.**

**AOD:** Yes

**Tap-zones:** No

**Language:** English, Russian
